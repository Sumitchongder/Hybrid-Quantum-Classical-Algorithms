# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a respectful, inclusive, and professional environment for all contributors, users, and collaborators involved in this project.

This repository focuses on **quantum algorithms, hybrid quantumâ€“classical systems, and high-performance simulation**, and therefore requires a high standard of technical integrity and professional conduct.

---

## Expected Behavior

All participants are expected to:

- Communicate respectfully and professionally
- Welcome diverse viewpoints, backgrounds, and expertise
- Provide constructive, evidence-based feedback
- Acknowledge limitations, assumptions, and prior work honestly
- Maintain scientific rigor and reproducibility

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment, discrimination, or offensive remarks
- Personal attacks or hostile communication
- Plagiarism or misrepresentation of results
- Publishing private or sensitive information without consent
- Any behavior that undermines scientific integrity or collaboration

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including:

- GitHub issues and pull requests
- Discussions and comments
- External communications representing the project

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may take appropriate action in response to violations, including:

- Issuing warnings
- Removing content
- Restricting or terminating participation
